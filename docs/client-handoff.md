## How to Manage Your Store

### Adding a New Product

1. Go to **Products → Add product**
2. Fill in title, description, price
3. Upload product photos (see Image Standards below)
4. In the **Tags** field, add relevant single-word tags
5. Product will automatically appear in the correct collection
6. Click **Save**

**Important:** You do NOT need to manually assign products to
collections. The tag system handles this automatically.

---

### Tag System

Use these single-word tags when adding products:

**Category tags (required — use at least one):**
- ring
- bracelet
- earring
- necklace

**Material tags (optional but recommended for SEO):**
- pearl
- gold
- silver
- amber
- turquoise

**Style tags (optional):**
- modern
- classic
- coin
- disc

**Example:** A pearl bracelet → tags: bracelet, pearl  
**Example:** A gold ring → tags: ring, gold  

---

### Image Standards

All product photos should follow this standard for consistent display:

- **Size:** 1440 x 1920 pixels (portrait ratio 3:4)
- **Format:** JPG for photos, PNG for transparent backgrounds
- **File size:** Under 1MB per image
- **Background:** Clean white or neutral background recommended
- **Quality:** High resolution, well lit, no blurry images

Photos that do not follow this standard may display inconsistently
in the collection grid.

### Changing the Announcement Bar

1. Go to **Online Store → Themes → Customize**
2. Click **Announcement bar** in the left sidebar
3. Edit the text directly
4. Click **Save**

Suggested uses: Free shipping offers, promotions, new arrivals,
seasonal messages.

---

### Managing Testimonials

1. Go to **Online Store → Themes → Customize**
2. Scroll to the **Testimonials** section on the homepage
3. Click any testimonial block to edit text and name
4. Use **Add block** to add more testimonials
5. Click **Save**

**Recommendation:** Only publish real customer reviews. Remove
placeholder reviews once you have real ones.

---

### Changing Collection Sort Order

1. Go to **Products → Collections**
2. Click the collection you want to edit
3. Drag and drop products into preferred order
4. First product in list appears first on the website
5. Changes save automatically

---

### Managing Navigation Menu

1. Go to **Content → Menus**
2. Click **Main Menu**
3. Add, edit, or remove items
4. Click **Save menu**

## Important Notes

### Shopify Plan
Your store is on the **Basic Shopify plan**. This is sufficient for
current needs. Consider upgrading if you need multiple regional
markets or advanced reporting.

### Domain
Keep your domain registrar account active and renew your domain
before it expires. Your domain is the foundation of your store.

### Backups
Before making any major changes to your theme, always duplicate it:
1. Go to **Online Store → Themes**
2. Click the three-dot menu on your active theme
3. Click **Duplicate**
4. This creates a safe backup you can restore if needed

### Making Changes
Some store settings can be difficult to reverse without technical
knowledge. Please reach out before making major configuration changes.
